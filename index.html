Perk force

I had the opportunity to contribute to the development of Perkforce, a platform designed to enhance employee engagement through a range of customizable perks and rewards. My role involved working extensively with modern web technologies to ensure a seamless and intuitive user experience. Utilizing my expertise in the MERN stack, I helped build and optimize the platform's functionality, ensuring robust performance and scalability. Evercare

I played a key role in the development of the Evercare website, including its comprehensive admin panel and mobile application. Utilizing my skills in the MERN stack and React Native, I contributed to creating a robust and user-friendly platform that streamlines healthcare management. For the web application, I focused on building a responsive and intuitive admin panel, enabling efficient management of healthcare services and patient interactions. On the mobile side, I worked on developing a seamless and engaging experience for users, ensuring accessibility and functionality across devices. My efforts were aimed at enhancing the overall user experience and operational efficiency for Evercare, ultimately supporting their mission to deliver exceptional healthcare services.
